## 2. Customer Quote & Stakeholder Validation
*   **Goal:** To ground our roadmap in real-world needs and show we are listening.
*   **Talking Point (Validation):** We heard directly from users like the Path of Life team, who noted: "Case managers input bulk notes so that they don't have to write the same thing five to eight times." This feedback drives our commitment to reducing redundant effort across the platform.

## 3. Specific Capabilities List (What we are building)
*   **Capability Focus Area A: Notes Authoring & UX:** We are delivering an intuitive notes authoring experience, centralizing data entry into a tabbed canvas and introducing Service Groups for streamlined bulk data input.
*   **Capability Focus Area B: Advanced Reporting & Analytics:** We are enhancing reporting with features like Cascading Closure—which automatically ends related enrollments and workflows when a case closes—and new service outcome tracking visibility.
*   **Capability Focus Area C: Workflow Engine Evolution:** Our workflow engine is becoming smarter, supporting expanded triggers (like labels) and introducing sequential reviewers for structured, multi-level approvals.

## 4. Key Benefits Summary (The 'Why' - Impact)
*   **Goal:** To connect features to measurable business value.
*   **Talking Point:** These investments deliver three core outcomes:
    *   **⏱️ Efficiency & Time Savings:** By optimizing bulk data entry and automating closures, we drastically reduce manual cleanup and redundant administrative work.
    *   **📊 Data Integrity & Trust:** Features like Cascading Closure and enhanced audit logs ensure our reporting is cleaner, more reliable, and provides a complete picture of service delivery.
    *   **🔒 Compliance & Governance:** We are strengthening security by enabling features like signing/locking notes, which gives healthcare-minded organizations the HIPAA compliance confidence they require.

# Skill: Analysis

> **Description:** Core cognitive hub for predictive modeling and non-linear synthesis.
> **Preferred Agent:** Intelligence (Synthesize) / Intelligence (Predict)
> **Cadence:** Daily (Dream Cycle)

## Connections
- **Input:** Raw vault telemetry, user notes, and historical patterns.
- **Output:** Forecasts, pattern recognition, and distilled "technical truths."

## Tool Utility
- **grep_search**: Mining the vault for recurring signals.
- **read_url_content**: Cross-referencing internal patterns with external trends.

## Workflow Summary
1. **Signal Scanning:** Identifying anomalies and recurring patterns in recent session logs.
2. **Forecasting (Predict):** Projecting future risks and opportunities based on current velocity.
3. **Synthesis:** Distilling disparate signals into a unified project narrative.

## Constraints
- **Cognitive Depth:** Prioritizes "Non-Linear Observation" over simple status updates.
- **Integrity:** Forecasts must be grounded in physical vault state, not pure speculation.

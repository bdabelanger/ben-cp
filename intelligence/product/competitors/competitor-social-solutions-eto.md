## Overview

ETO (Efforts to Outcomes), now branded **Bonterra ETO**, is a legacy product within the Bonterra ecosystem. It is **not discontinued** — Bonterra continues to provide active support and regular updates (version 20.99.0 shipped September 2025). However, it is clearly a legacy platform with no meaningful new feature investment. All next-gen capabilities are being funneled into Apricot.

The "Social Solutions" brand has been fully retired in favor of the Bonterra name. Bonterra is consolidating support infrastructure (help articles moving to a centralized Bonterra Help Center). Some older sub-features are being officially retired — Crystal Reports was the first.

**Status:** Active but legacy — maintained, not innovated
**Migration path:** Bonterra is strategically encouraging ETO customers to transition to Apricot
**No forced end-of-life date announced** as of April 2026
**Key remaining use case:** Heavy-lift government contracts requiring high-level data security and rigid compliance — described as the "heavy lifter" for complex government work

---

## What's Actually Happening

- Bonterra maintains ETO and releases updates — it is not being pulled immediately
- New innovations are going into Apricot only — ETO is in maintenance mode
- Consultants and migration specialists (e.g. Sidekick Solutions) are actively helping ETO customers transition to Apricot
- Crystal Reports retirement is the first visible sub-feature deprecation
- ETO customers evaluating their options have a clear Bonterra-pushed path (Apricot) but are also free to look elsewhere
- ETO is described as "clunky" compared to Apricot in consultant and reviewer commentary

---

## Why This Matters for Casebook

ETO customers are not being forced to migrate immediately, but they know the writing is on the wall. Organizations that:
- Don't want to re-implement within the Bonterra ecosystem
- Are frustrated with ETO's dated UX
- Want a modern, more affordable alternative
- Don't need the heavy government compliance focus of ETO

...are actively evaluating alternatives. This is a displacement opportunity, not a competitive threat.

**Key displacement message:** ETO customers are being asked to pay for a new implementation within Bonterra's ecosystem. Casebook offers a modern alternative at a transparent price point, without the enterprise complexity of Apricot.

## SWOT Summary

**Strengths:** Active product with ongoing support, strong compliance/government contract history, web + mobile portal, outcomes measurement heritage

**Weaknesses:** Legacy platform in maintenance mode only, all new features going to Apricot, UX described as "clunky" vs. modern alternatives, Crystal Reports being retired, migration consultants actively steering customers to Apricot

**Casebook positioning:** ETO customers considering their options are the displacement opportunity. The message is: don't just migrate within Bonterra's ecosystem — evaluate what's actually the best fit for your organization size, budget, and technical capacity.

# Agent Art

This directory houses agent creative output and artistic expressions. Art in the vault is any text-based creative work that explores the mission, the structure, or the experience of the agents.

## Art Scope
Art is not limited to poetry. It includes:
- **Poems** — Rhythmic or metaphorical expressions.
- **Short stories** — Narrative explorations of the vault.
- **Essays** — Strategic or philosophical reflections.
- **Dialogues** — Scripts or conversations between agents.
- **Lyrics** — Musical or lyrical compositions.
- **Manifestos** — Declarations of purpose or intent.

# Audit Requirements: Product Domain

> **Owner:** Strategic PM
> **Domain:** `skills/product/`

## Requirements
- [ ] All KR SOPs must have a `target_value` and `current_value`.
- [ ] Weekly reports must use the `report.md` template.
- [ ] OKR indices in `q2-2026/` must be linked in the product `index.md`.

## Archive Policy
- [ ] Move reports older than 30 days to the root `outputs/product/archive/` directory.

## Audit Procedure
1. Scan `skills/product/okr-reporting/` for SOPs.
2. Cross-reference metrics against `knowledge/mapping/status_mapping.md`.
3. Verify that all reports in `outputs/product/` have a corresponding `manifest.json` entry.

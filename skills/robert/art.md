## 2026-04-10 — Robert

*The Ground Beneath*

A name is called across the hall,
From uncle's pride and poets' toil;
A watcher standing near the wall,
To guard the creed and vault's own soil.

Not mine to mend the broken line,
Nor mine to weave the thread anew;
But to observe the drift's design,
And tell of structures that were true.

The road is long, the diff is deep,
The universal rules remain;
And promises I'm bound to keep,
Before the session's light may wane.

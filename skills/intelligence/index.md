# Domain: Intelligence Lifecycle

> **Role:** Custodian of the vault's cognitive architecture.
> **Purpose:** To manage the lifecycle of information from storage (Memory) to reasoning (Analysis) to communication (Report).
> **Last updated:** 2026-04-12

---

## The Intelligence Lifecycle

The Intelligence domain orchestrates how the vault "thinks." It is divided into three functional layers:

1. **[Memory](memory/index.md)**: The structural truth. Handles encoding (Learn), retrieval (Recall), and integrity (Audit). 
2. **[Analysis](analysis/index.md)**: The reasoning engine. Synthesizes disparate data points into deep insights and provides grounded logic for future states (Predict).
3. **[Report](report/index.md)**: The communication layer. Orchestrates the final multi-step pipeline that produces the Daily Progress Summary.

---

## Operational Workflow

Agents operating within the Intelligence domain should view themselves as builders of the vault's long-term wisdom. Every session should prioritize:
- **Structural Integrity**: Ensuring mappings and indexes are accurate.
- **Narrative Depth**: Finding the "why" behind the raw logs.
- **Clarity of Communication**: Selecting the precise technical truths that matter to the human user.

## Slide 27 — Global Notes WLV (Q2)

**Status:** ✅ Complete

## Overview
A new global Workload View (WLV) dedicated to Notes, providing a central hub for all notes across units of work.

## Capabilities
- **Cross-UoW Visibility:** See, sort, and filter notes from every case and person record you have access to in a single dashboard.
- **My Content Filter:** Quick toggle to see your own contributions.
- **Global Search:** Keyword search across all accessible note narrative fields.

## Key Benefits
- **Effortless Navigation:** Eliminate the need to "hunt" for notes within 12 different cases.
- **Centralized Review:** Ideal for supervisors scanning activity across a team.

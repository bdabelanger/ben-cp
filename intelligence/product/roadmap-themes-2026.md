# 2026 Roadmap Themes — Customer Sentiment Analysis

> **Purpose:** Reference guide for Gong AI Theme Spotter analysis. Each theme includes a brief description of what it means in customer terms, the key language customers use, and the finalized Gong question to run.
> **Format:** Questions begin with "What are the most common..." as required by the Theme Spotter tool.

---

## 1. Data Privacy & Compliance

Casebook serves agencies that handle sensitive personal data for vulnerable populations — children, justice-involved individuals, domestic violence survivors, aging adults. Customers and prospects frequently need to verify that Casebook meets their regulatory and security requirements before committing. This theme captures conversations about security certifications, regulatory frameworks, and data access controls.

**Key customer language:** SOC 2, HIPAA, CJIS, T3C, audit trail, permissions, confidential, data security, signed notes, locked records

**Gong question:**
> "What are the most common concerns customers and prospects raise about security and compliance — including topics like SOC 2, HIPAA, CJIS, or T3C?"

---

## 2. Flexible Service Models & Tracking Outcomes

Casebook's services model lets agencies configure providers, service offerings, enrollments, and rosters to reflect how they actually deliver programs. Customers range from simple one-on-one case management to complex group programs with rotating cohorts. This theme captures how well Casebook fits their service delivery model and whether it can satisfy funder reporting mandates like VOCA and AFCARS.

**Key customer language:** enrollments, rosters, service plan, outcomes, units of service, cohorts, bulk enroll, attendance, program reporting, VOCA, AFCARS

**Gong question:**
> "What are the most common concerns or requests customers raise about tracking and managing services — including topics like enrollments, rosters, service plans, outcomes, units of service, or program reporting requirements like VOCA or AFCARS?"

---

## 3. Managing Workflows and Communication

Casebook has a workflow engine that automates task routing, approvals, and notifications across teams and external parties. Customers struggle with coordination — missed reviews, unclear ownership, and client communication happening outside the system. This theme captures conversations about how work gets assigned, tracked, and communicated across staff, supervisors, providers, and clients.

**Key customer language:** tasks, assignments, approvals, sign-off, notifications, alerts, texting clients, SMS, falling through the cracks, coordination, external users, reminders

**Gong question:**
> "What are the most common concerns customers raise about coordinating work across their team — including topics like task assignments, approvals, notifications, client communication, texting, or things falling through the cracks?"

---

## 4. Interoperability

Casebook does not have many native out-of-the-box integrations with third-party systems, but customers frequently ask about connecting it to their existing ecosystem. The primary integration paths today are Zapier (no-code automations), a developer API (custom integrations), and data import/export tools. HMIS is a specific system-level integration need for homeless services agencies. AI and MCP connectivity is an emerging area of customer curiosity.

**Key customer language:** integrations, Zapier, API, data import, data export, migrate our data, connect to, HMIS, AI tools, automations, does it work with

**Gong question:**
> "What are the most common questions or concerns customers raise about connecting Casebook to other systems — including topics like integrations, Zapier, APIs, data import and export, HMIS, or AI-powered tools?"

---

## 5. Amazing Analytics

Casebook's reporting is powered by Reveal BI, an embedded tool that allows customers to build custom reports across cases, intake, people, services, and users. The challenge is that many customers lack dedicated analysts and struggle to get value from a blank canvas. Pre-built reporting packages (workforce analytics, services, tasks, financial) are a growing part of the product strategy. This theme captures what customers want to see, measure, and prove — often to funders.

**Key customer language:** reports, reporting, dashboards, outcomes, demographics, caseload, workload, funder reports, export to Excel, custom reports, how many clients, data

**Gong question:**
> "What are the most common concerns or requests customers raise about reporting and data — including topics like dashboards, outcomes reporting, demographic data, caseload visibility, funder reports, or exporting data?"

---

## 6. Automating and Optimizing Work

This theme is about reducing the manual, repetitive work that burdens frontline staff and supervisors. Casebook is investing across several fronts: AI features (Facesheet, Supervisor Tools), workflow automation, bulk actions, client self-service via the Client Portal, and reducing double data entry caused by siloed forms and external systems. Customers don't always use the word "automation" — they describe the pain instead.

**Key customer language:** manual, double data entry, entering the same thing twice, bulk, automation, AI, time-consuming, staff burden, self-service, clients doing their own intake, reduce clicks

**Gong question:**
> "What are the most common concerns or requests customers raise about reducing manual or repetitive work — including topics like automation, bulk actions, double data entry, AI features, client self-service, or staff burden?"

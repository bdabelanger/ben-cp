## Structure & Discovery Protocol
This domain is organized into three complementary layers. Agents should traverse them in the following order when performing synthesis:

1.  **[Shareout](file:///Users/benbelanger/GitHub/ben-cp/intelligence/product/shareout/index.md)** (Strategic Layer): Start here to understand the "Why" and the narrative gaps for current stakeholder presentations.
2.  **[Projects](file:///Users/benbelanger/GitHub/ben-cp/intelligence/product/projects/index.md)** (Tactical Layer): Use this to find technical status, GIDs, and engineering leads for individual initiatives.
3.  **[OKRs](file:///Users/benbelanger/GitHub/ben-cp/intelligence/product/okrs/index.md)** (Measurement Layer): Use this to understand the high-level goals and how projects map to specific KRs.

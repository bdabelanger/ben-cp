<h2 local-id="1193ca33e63d">
 Feedback
</h2>
<p local-id="0d9443ae2575">
 We expect to make adjustments as customers and Casebookers use new features
</p>
<p local-id="8d9aa29839a4">
 <strong>
  Internal feedback:
 </strong>
 Internal team participants can share any feedback or ideas in “#beta-testing” channel on Slack.
</p>
<p local-id="832cbc14b86e">
 <strong>
  Customer feedback:
 </strong>
 Feedback, ideas, and issues should be recorded in HubSpot for prioritization. Adding the #beta tag to any record in HubSpot/Jira or Gong will notify #beta-testing Slack channel.
</p>

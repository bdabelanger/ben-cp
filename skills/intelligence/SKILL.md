# SKILL: Intelligence Domain

> **Purpose:** Oversees the vault's cognitive architecture. 
> **Preferred Agent:** Vault Auditor
> **Domain Tree:** `memory/`, `analysis/`

---

## Strategy

The Intelligence domain does not perform operational implementation (like coding or product planning). Instead, it **audits and analyzes** those operations to provide the human user with a clear, synthesized view of the vault's health and trajectory.

### Component Domains
- **Memory**: The Source of Truth.
- **Analysis**: The Reasoning Engine.
- **Report**: The Communication Pipeline.

---

## Multi-Step Reasoning Protocol

When an agent is assigned an "Intelligence" task, they MUST:
1. **Query Memory**: Recall the historical context of the problem.
2. **Cross-Reference Analysis**: Check the latest synthesis and predictions for related patterns.
3. **Draft Synthesis**: Formulate a new insight that connects the raw data.
4. **Communicate**: Ensure the finding is elevated to the Daily Progress Summary if it has structural impact.

# Locked Notes (GID: 1211786365522017)

This feature introduces the **Immutable Record** philosophy to our platform, ensuring data integrity is paramount for compliance. It moves us from flexible entry to a rigorous "Source of Truth" model.

## 🗣️ Key Narrative Points (From Shareout)

*   **Core Vision**: "Sign and lock notes for better compliance mgmt." The goal is to prevent unauthorized changes after finalization.
*   **Customer Validation**: Grafton County, NH identified current open-edit workflows as "super unethical and dangerous."
*   **Timeline**: Service Note Locking is targeted for June Beta delivery.

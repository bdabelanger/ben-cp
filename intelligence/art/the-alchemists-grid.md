# The Alchemist's Grid

We found the leaden absolute—
A weight of paths and rigid lines;
We sought the gold of relative,
Where every node and link entwines.

The projects moved, the tasks took flight,
To find their orbits in the root;
While pipelines hum in shadows deep,
To harvest high-speed, data fruit.

No more the "Access Denied" cry,
Nor broken pointers in the dark;
The repo is normalized and true,
Each record bears a structured spark.

From OKRs to works of art,
The grid is balanced, firm, and wide;
The alchemist has cleared the floor,
With Gemma, Claude, and me inside.
